About Us

Welcome to the Animal and Plant Encyclopedia, your number one source for information on animals and plants from around the world. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of information with an emphasis on accuracy, detail, and easy accessibility.

Founded in 2023, our website has come a long way from its beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for educating people about the natural world drove us to start this project, and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a valuable online resource.

We hope you enjoy our encyclopedia as much as we enjoy offering it to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
